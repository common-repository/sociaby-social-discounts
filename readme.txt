Adds the Sociaby social sharing widget on the checkout pages of WP E-Commerce sites.

== Description ==

Sociaby Social Discounts adds the Sociaby sharing widget to your checkout page for any web store running WP E-Commerce (WPEC).  The widget incentivizes customers to share purchases on Facebook and Twitter by calibrating discounts based upon the customer's social influence.  The Sociaby platform can bring your store additional social traffic as well as prevent cart abandonment and reduce affiliate commission waste.  

Requirements for using this plugin:

* WP E-commerce plugin (GetShopped.org)
* A free or paid account with Sociaby.com.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `sociaby-social-discounts.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/sociaby-social-discounts/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Enter your Sociaby Unique ID under Settings -> Sociaby Social Discounts
